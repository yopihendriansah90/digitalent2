Vision & Mission

Direction, operating principles, and long-term purpose behind DigiTalent.

Halaman ini merangkum visi jangka panjang dan misi operasional DigiTalent dalam mendukung transformasi digital melalui talenta yang unggul, inovatif, dan siap industri.

Vision

To be the leading strategic partner in developing and providing superior, innovative, and globally competitive digital talent to support international-standard digital transformation.

What It Means

DigiTalent positions itself as a long-term partner that not only prepares digital professionals, but also helps organizations secure the right talent, the right capability model, and the right operational mindset for sustainable transformation.

Strategic Direction

Superior Talent

Developing professionals with strong capability, quality, and readiness for real-world execution.

Innovative Delivery

Building adaptive systems for training, recruitment, and technology services that remain relevant to evolving industry needs.

Global Competitiveness

Aligning digital talent development with international standards and business expectations.
